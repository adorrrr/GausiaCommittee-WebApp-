Gausia Committee Bangladesh

Gausia Committee Bangladesh is a non-profit organization that works to provide support to the underprivileged people of Bangladesh. We are a group of volunteers who are dedicated to helping the people in need. Our mission is to provide food, shelter, and education to the people who are in need. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to live a better life and we are here to help them achieve that.

Together Let's make a change

Flood Emergency

About 26,000 sq km (18%) of Bangladesh is flooded by monsoons every year. At this time, especially in the northern part of the country, the flood-affected people are the victims of extreme misery. Every year, the Gausia Committee Bangladesh carries out relief activities in the flood-hit districts of the country and takes the initiative to stand by the needy and helpless people.

Winter Appeal

One of the invaluable teachings of Islam is human service. Every year, the Gausia Committee Bangladesh organizes a 'Winter Clothing Distribution and Da'wah Program' to bring a little warmth to the helpless cold-stricken people in remote areas of Bangladesh.

Ramadan Ifter Fund

One of the regular programs of the Gausia Committee Bangladesh is the distribution of Iftar and Ramadan food. The Foundation has taken initiative to distribute Iftar items among the needy fasting people in remote areas of the country so that they can observe fasting and worship without any hindrance during the month of Ramadan.
